What is Organizational Security?

Understanding and reacting to risks at an intentional, organizational level -- and as a community

How do we respond? Better yet - How can we prepare? * Many aspects of risk, including physical, legal, psycho-social - which we have heard today already. I am focused on the digital

Digital Security is Dynamic - not a checklist

Don't open email attachments

Minimize travel to high-risk locations

Avoid protests, political unrest

Minimize social media engagement

OrgSec is also when I advise most orgs we end up looking at a checklist like (FRAGS): ... Obviously this doesn't easily transfer to journalists, where this list of don'ts is basically their job description.

But -- Why ?

Keep a story private until it's ready

Keep a story online once it's out

Protect staff from repercussions

Protect sources - and attract high-risk ones

Protect readers and your reputation.

Security is complicated and costly - and we've made it this far... Security success is very quiet - but so are many security failures, and luck, if you're not looking.
